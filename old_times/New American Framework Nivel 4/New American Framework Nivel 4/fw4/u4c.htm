  5. I'm not sure. Perhaps it's symbolic of the passing of an era.

  3. Do you like this one?

  4. Well, what I like about it is the composition, the way you can see two views at the same time.

  5. They're really nice—a balance of soft warm tones and plenty of light.

  6. Yes, I love it.
