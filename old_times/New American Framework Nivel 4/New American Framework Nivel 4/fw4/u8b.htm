Yes, well, um... the problem concerns my partner, the person I live with. Normally we gets along really well together, I mean, we have a good relationship, but recently he's been very busy at work and usually comes home really tired. And all he wants to do is sit on the sofa and watch televsion, or go to bed and fall asleep reading a book. I mean, this is fine, but, but not every day. I work too, but I also like to go out sometimes, you know, to the movies or with friends. When we do go out, which is not very often, he is never very happy, you know, he sits in a corner, looking at his watch, always asking when we can go home. I like talking to people and dancing and having fun, but I'm finding it more and more difficult these days, and I think I would feel guilty if I went out without him. I think he should come out with me and enjoy himself. I don't know. What do think I should do?
