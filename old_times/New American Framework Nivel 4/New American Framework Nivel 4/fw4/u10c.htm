Vague language

1. Order these conversations, by numbering the sentences.

1) In a hardware store

  • Sorry? I don't understand.
  • You know, that liquid you pour in the sink to unclog the drain.
  • Yes. I'd like some of that stuff for unclogging the sink.
  • A plunger. This rubber thing with a handle. It's cheaper than Mr. Muscle.
  • Oh, you mean Mr. Muscle. Why don't you try it with a plunger first?
  • A what?
  • Oh, that's what those are for. I think my friend has one of those. Thanks anyway.
  • Can I help you?

2) Later, at home

  • I don't know, a strange object, It looks like a kind of tool.
  • Oh yeah? What did he leave?
  • Right. That was Frank, and it's not a tool. It's just one of those things for unclogging the sink.
  • There was a man here a minute ago. He left something for you on the table.
