Going on a trip, what every traveler should know.

If you're traveling independently there are a number of things you're going to need. Here's a brief guide.

Most important is a good tent 1 . You'll also need a sleeping bag 2—it can get very cold at night and a carry mat 3 to put under the sleeping bag. Hot food and drink is another great way of keeping warm. For this you'll need a stove 4 for cooking and some pots and pans 5 for putting water and food in to heat . Don't forget to take
matches 6 to light the stove!

If you're going to be walking a lot you'll certainly need a good pair of boots 7 . A waterproof jacket 8 will also be very useful when the weather is bad.

What about emergencies? A first-aid kit 9 is very important for injuries or illnesses and a sewing kit 10 can be used to mend clothes. Many problems are caused by getting lost so take a map 11 and a compass 12 . If you do have problems you can use your whistle 13—the emergency signal is to blow it six times.

And finally, a good backpack 14 to carry all of this in!
