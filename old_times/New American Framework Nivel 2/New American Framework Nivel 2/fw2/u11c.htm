3. Shopping Language

What do we say when

a. ...we want to buy something?1. Can I pay by credit card/with cash?
b. ...we want a different size?2. It suits me/you
c. ...we want to put the clothes on to see if we like them?3. It fits.
d. ...we want to say that it is the right size?4. I'll take it/them.
e. ...we want to say that it looks good?5. I'd like...
f. ...we decide to buy something?6. Can I try it/them on?
g. ...we want to know how to pay?7. Do you have a smaller/larger size?
