Adverbs of frequency

1. Adverbs of degree.

  • I am enjoying the unit very much.

    Sentences with enjoy, like, don't like + adverb follow a particular order:

  • I am enjoying the unit very much

    subject + verb + object + adverb

    You can also use really for emphasis.

    Really goes before the main verb:

  • I am really enjoying the unit.
  • I don't really like the Rolling Stones.
