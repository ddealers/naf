5. In Transit

Might for possibility

1. might to express possibility

You use might (not) + infinitive to say that something now or in the future is possible:

  • Elvis might be alive. =It's possible that Elvis is alive
  • Elvis might not be dead. =It's possible that Elvis is not dead
  • Elvis might live in New York. =It's possible that Elvis lives in New York
