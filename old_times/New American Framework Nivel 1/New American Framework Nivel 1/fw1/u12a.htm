A biography of John Lennon

1. Giving biographical information.

Read this biography of John Lennon, and answer the question:
How old was he when he died?

John Lennon was born on October 9, 1940. He left school when he was 16 and went to Liverpool Art College. At art college he formed a group called The Quarrymen. This group later became The Beatles. In 1962 he got married to Cynthia Powell, who was in his class at art school. Their son, Julian, was born in 1963. In 1966 John met Yoko Ono at an art gallery in 1966 and in 1968 John and Cynthia got divorced. John got married to Yoko Ono on March 20, 1969 and they went to live in New York in 1971. John separated from Yoko Ono in 1974, but the couple got back together again in 1975. Their son, Sean, was born on October 9 1976. John Lennon died on December 9, 1980. Someone shot him in front of his apartment.

How old was he when he died?

Answer: He was 40 when he died.
