1. Adjectives of Place

Mexican food is on the menu.
Mexican = from Mexico

There are different types of Adjectives of Place;

ones that end with -an Mexican (from Mexico)
ones that end with -ish Spanish (from Spain)
ones that end with -ese Japanese (from Japan)
irregular ones Thai (from Thailand)
