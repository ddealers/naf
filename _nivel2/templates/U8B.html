8. Gossip

A good luck story

1. You are going to listen to a story. But first, check you know the meaning of these words

  1. flat tire
  2. tire
  3. spare tire
  4. tools

2. Now listen to the story. Then put the pictures in the right order.

3. Listen again and say if these sentences are true or false.

  1. The doctor didn't have a spare tire.

  2. The man stopped and helped him change the tire.

  3. The man wanted money.

  4. The man took the doctor's tools and wine.

  5. The man gave the doctor a lottery ticket.

  6. The lottery ticket won first prize.

  7. The doctor collected the money.

  8. The man was sorry that he took the doctor's things.

  9. The doctor told reporters "I am not a thief."
