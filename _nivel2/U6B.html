    5. Complete this chart of adjective, comparative and superlative forms. There are 4 different types.

    Short Adjectives (1 syllable)
    adjective comparative superlative
    big bigger than the (1)
    hot (2) than the hottest
    small (3) than the smallest
    (4) cheaper than the (5)
    2 Syllable Adjectives that end with "y"
    adjective comparative superlative
    pretty prettier than the prettiest
    happy happier than the (6)
    dirty (7) than the dirtiest
    (8) healthier than the (9)
    Long Adjectives (2 syllables or more)
    adjective comparative superlative
    beautiful more beautiful than the most beautiful
    modern more modern than the (10)
    expensive (11) than the most expensive
    (12) more dangerous than (13)
    Irregular Adjectives
    adjective comparative superlative
    good better than the (14)
    (15) worse than the worst
    far farther than the farthest
