6. Sentence topic passive

Look at these sentences:

Néel and Yongden spent two months in Lhasa.
They visited the Potala Palace.

The subject of the first sentence (Néel and Yongden) is also the subject of the second sentence (they).

Four times Néel tried to make the journey.
Each time she was discovered and sent back.

In the second example, Néel is the subject of the first sentence. And she is also the subject of the second sentence. The passive (was discovered) is used in the second sentence to continue talking about Néel.
